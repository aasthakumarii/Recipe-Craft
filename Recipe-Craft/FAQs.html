Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

This website provides users with recipes based on ingredients they have, along with various features like sorting by calories and surprise recipe suggestions.

How can I search for a recipe?

You can search for recipes by entering the ingredients you have in the search bar, and the website will suggest recipes based on that input.

Can I sort recipes by calories?

Yes, you can sort the recipes by calories using the sorting options in the filter section.

What is the "Surprise Me!" feature?

The "Surprise Me!" feature shows a random recipe suggestion when you click the button, offering a fun way to discover new recipes.

Is the website free to use?

Yes, the website is free to use for all users without any subscription or payment required.
